About Environ

Our Mission

Environ is dedicated to empowering individuals and organizations to take action against environmental violations. We believe in creating a transparent, accessible platform for anonymous reporting that protects whistleblowers and the planet.

What We Do

We provide a secure way to report environmental harm. This reporting site allows for users to, anonymously or not, inform environmental organizations and regulators alike on environmental harm near them. We are committed to fostering change and holding violators accountable.
