Welcome to Environ

Environ is a platform for environmental whistleblowing. Our mission is to empower individuals to take action against environmental violations by providing an anonymous way to report incidents. Whether you're witnessing illegal dumping, pollution, or any other harm to the environment, Environ is here to help make your voice heard.

Ready to make a difference? Get started by logging in with your Google account.

Login With Google

If you do not want to log in, feel free to submit an anonymous report

Features of Environ

  • Anonymous Reporting: Concerned about privacy? You have the option to submit reports anonymously and protect your identity.
  • User Authentication: Securely log in through Google to manage your reports and stay updated on the actions taken in response to your submissions.
  • Admin Oversight: Admin users can review submitted reports and approve them

Together, we can make a significant impact on preserving our planet for future generations!
